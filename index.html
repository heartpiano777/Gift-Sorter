Gift Sorter

story

It’s just before Christmas at Santa Claus’s house. Santa and his reindeer are very busy sorting all the presents. You’ve been asked by Santa to help with the sorting. Let’s start organizing the presents one after another!

How to Play

You will be given three pieces of information as a task: the type of toy, the number of toys, and the color of the present box. Drag and drop the toys according to the task and place them into the present box! Once you’ve placed all the toys inside, the present box will be wrapped, and Santa will be delighted! The time limit is 60 seconds. Let’s sort as many presents as we can within the time limit.
